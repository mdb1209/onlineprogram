The Rhythm

How Your Coaching Works

1

Get set up

Open your program above and read through the training split, your macro targets, and how the tracker works. This is your home base for the next 90 days.

2

Train the plan

Follow the 5-day program and log your workouts, meals, and habits as you go. Consistency is the whole game. You bring the commitment, I bring the system.

3

Book your in-person sessions

You get two sessions with me each month. Reserve them through the CultureFit booking link, same place you book classes. Details below.

4

Send your weekly check-in

Every Sunday, email me your check-in: bodyweight, how training felt, your wins, and where you struggled. Photos are optional but they help me see what the scale can't.

5

I adjust your plan

I review every check-in and tweak your training and macros so you keep moving forward. You'll hear back from me within 48 hours with your updates for the week ahead.

Your In-Person Sessions

Two sessions a month, one-on-one with me. This is where the hybrid part earns its name. We use the time for what a screen can't do:

  • Form and technique coaching on your big lifts
  • Hands-on cues so you train safer and harder
  • Body composition tracking and measurements
  • Real-time adjustments to your program
