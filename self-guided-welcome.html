Your Game Plan

How To Win On Your Own

1

Know your numbers

Start in the macro section and lock in your daily targets. This is the foundation everything else is built on. Five minutes now saves you weeks of guessing.

2

Train the plan

Follow the 5-day program exactly as written for the first few weeks before you start tweaking. Trust the structure. It's designed to build on itself.

3

Track everything

Your tracker saves right in your browser, so your streak, measurements, strength log, and habits are waiting for you every time you come back. What gets tracked gets done.

4

Chase the streak

Don't aim for perfect, aim for consistent. Check off your daily habits and watch the streak grow. Momentum is the whole game, and it compounds fast.

5

Make it yours

Once the rhythm clicks, lean on the meal swaps and grocery lists to fit the plan around your real life. The best program is the one you'll actually stick to.
